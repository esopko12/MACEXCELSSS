EXCEL/Student Support Services is an academic support program that serves low-income, first-generation, and students with disabilities.

What We Offer
• Broad tutoring support in most courses, in-person and online
• Advising services oriented toward course selection, degree planning, and transferring
• Over $15,000 in scholarships and Supplemental Grant Aid awarded annually
• Learning Workshops providing supplemental academic support
• Campus tours and educational enrichment events
• Financial aid and economic literacy information
